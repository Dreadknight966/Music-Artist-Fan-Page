Biography

Indie band “Mili” consists of: Canadian vocalist Cassie Wei, capable of writing lyrics in three languages. Composer and arranger Yamato Kasai, with a proven track record of providing music to various artists, such as Higuchi Ai, DAOKO, and Hanazawa Kana. Bassist Yukihito and drummer Yoshida Shoto are long-time musicians who have gone through both indie and major debuts. Animator, illustrator, and character designer Ao Fujimori, an experienced animation director, is in charge of Mili's visual artwork.

Mili has written music for popular anime and game titles such as "Ghost in the Shell", "Goblin Slayer", "Limbus Company", "Library of Ruina", "ENDER LILIES", and "Promise of Wizard". They create music in various genres, such as classical, electronic, rock, and jazz, while maintaining a high level of quality. They handle music, visual art, merchandise, management, and business all by themselves. Mili is fully in control of their production and is recognized as a unique presence both domestically and internationally.

In 2023, Mili will celebrate their 11th year since their formation and will tour Poland, the US, and China. They are also producing many songs for anime, game, and IT companies in Japan, Korea, China, Canada, and Poland.
